---
title: Labels and Self-Definition
description: A prose poem on how self-chosen labels fail to capture authentic identity.
date: 2026-03-09
---

by Mark Ayers, 2024

We seek labels to wear—to place upon our brow—to say to ourselves and to others, "Yes. This is me." And in time, we realize: "I am not, was not, what this label means. This label I chose to wear."
