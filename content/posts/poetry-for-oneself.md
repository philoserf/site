---
title: Poetry for Oneself
description: A 1986 humorous poem by Mark Ayers on writing poetry purely for personal enjoyment.
date: 2026-03-10
---

by Mark Ayers, 1986

Poetry that's written  
To entertain oneself

Has a value of its own  
Just like a little elf

Who putters about  
And picks his toes  
And doesn't have a care

Who runs around  
Without clothes  
In the morning air.
