---
title: Poetry for Oneself
description: A short humorous poem by Mark Ayers (1986) reflecting on the intrinsic value of writing poetry purely for one's own enjoyment and entertainment.
date: 2026-03-10
---

by Mark Ayers, 1986

Poetry that's written  
To entertain oneself

Has a value of its own  
Just like a little elf

Who putters about  
And picks his toes  
And doesn't have a care

Who runs around  
Without clothes  
In the morning air.
