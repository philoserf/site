---
title: Dating Age Range Formula
description: Mathematical formula defining acceptable dating age ranges using the half-your-age-plus-seven rule.
date: 2026-03-13
math: true
---

For ages $n \geq n_{\min}$ (cultural), acceptable partner ages fall in $\left[\max\!\left(\frac{n}{2} + 7, n_{\min}\right), 2n - 14\right]$.
