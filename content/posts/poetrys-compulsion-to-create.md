---
title: Poetry's Compulsion to Create
description: A 1984 poem by Mark Ayers on the compulsion to write despite poetry's vast surplus over readership.
date: 2026-03-26
---

by Mark Ayers, 1984

More poetry is written  
Than ever will be read.

Still, we apply pen to paper.

Scratch and slide  
Words unfold  
Are found.

Melody in voice,  
Expressed.
