My critical foci for a happy, healthy, productive life are these: sleep, move, eat, grow, play, and share.

- Set a specific time for reading each day. Make sure to pick a quiet, distraction-free environment.
- Take your time while reading, and make sure to take notes. Review your notes after you finish a book to help you retain the information.
